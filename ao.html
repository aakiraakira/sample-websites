Understanding the craft.

What makes ÀO different from traditional Baijiu?
ÀO is crafted using a unique blend of five organic grains and fermented in ancient clay pits, but it undergoes a proprietary filtration process that smooths out the "fire" often associated with Baijiu, resulting in a cleaner, more floral finish suited for the international palate.
How should I serve ÀO?
We recommend serving ÀO neat at room temperature to fully appreciate its complex aroma. However, our Jade expression is excellent over a large block of ice or used as a base for high-end cocktails.
