Membership FAQ

Who is BTC Bollywood for?
We are a members-only club targeted at affluent professionals aged 35-55. Our community consists primarily of bankers, traders, commodity experts, and family office executives looking for a sophisticated environment to unwind and network.
How do I get an invite?
Membership is by invitation or referral only. However, you may submit a request for access via the form below. Our committee reviews applications weekly.
Is there a dress code?
Yes. The dress code is Smart Casual / Business Chic. We maintain a polished atmosphere befitting our clientele.
Can I book the club for corporate events?
Absolutely. We offer corporate table bookings and full-venue buyouts for private functions. Please contact us directly for rates and availability.
