Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining the pack.

What defines a "small dog"?
We generally accept breeds such as Poodles, Pomeranians, Maltipoos, Corgis, and similar sizes up to 10-12kg. If you are unsure, please WhatsApp us for a quick check!
What vaccinations are required?
For the safety of all pups, your dog must have up-to-date core vaccinations (Distemper, Parvovirus, Hepatitis) and ideally Kennel Cough protection.
How does the 1:5 ratio work?
We assign one dedicated staff member to oversee a maximum of five dogs. This ensures every dog gets supervised play, rest, and immediate attention if needed.
Can I bring my dog's own food?
Absolutely! If your dog has a specific diet or lunch requirement, pack it in a labeled container and we will feed them during rest time.
