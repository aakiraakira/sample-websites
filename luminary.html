Frequently Asked Questions

What is the timeline for setting up a business in Singapore?
For standard private limited companies, the actual incorporation can be done within 1-3 business days once all documents are prepared. However, bank account opening and visa processing may take an additional 2-4 weeks. We guide you through every step to expedite this.
Do you handle Employment Passes (EP) for foreign staff?
Yes. As part of our advisory, we assess candidate eligibility, prepare the necessary documentation, and apply for Employment Passes, S-Passes, and Dependant Passes for your relocated team members and family.
What industries do you specialize in for recruitment?
We have a strong network in Finance/FinTech, Logistics/Supply Chain, and Technology. We focus primarily on mid-to-senior level executive search (Managers, Directors, C-Suite).
Can you help with student accommodation?
Yes, beyond university placement, we offer a "settling-in" service for students which includes finding suitable accommodation, setting up bank accounts, and orientation to life in Singapore.
