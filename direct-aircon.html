Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my aircon?

For residential usage, we recommend a general service every 3 months. If you use it every night, quarterly servicing ensures the air remains clean and the unit efficient.

What is the difference between Chemical Wash and Overhaul?

A Chemical Wash is done with the unit on the wall using a bag to catch water. An Overhaul involves dismantling the entire fan coil unit to wash individual parts thoroughly. Overhaul is recommended for very old or heavily choked units.

Do you provide a warranty?

Yes, we provide a 90-day workmanship warranty on our servicing and repairs. Parts replacement warranties vary by manufacturer.

Why is my aircon leaking water?

Leaking is usually caused by a choked drainage pipe, dirty filters, or insufficient gas causing ice formation. Our troubleshooting service can identify the exact cause.
