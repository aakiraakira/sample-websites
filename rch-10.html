A RICHZ

Inspired Integrated Sanctuary for 3-Generation Coexistence Living,Working, Learning and Wellbeing.

P'Maveraz - Spring Forth Sanctuary is a transformative, GBI Platinum-certified green community reimagining contemporary living through LOHAS principles. Anchored by mineral-rich hot springs in a nature-rich environment, it integrates resort living, international education, wellness hospitality, and ecological stewardship across five thoughtfully curated zones of holistic growth.

P'Maveraz - The RICHZ Master Development Zones

R

Zone R - Residential Suites

Refined residences offering tranquil living amid forested landscapes,designed for comfort, rejuvenation and long-term wellbeing.

I

Zone I - College University & International Forest Boarding Schools

A purpose-built international education ecosystem, from nursery to Level 12,where learning is immersed in nature, and sustainability is embedded into daily life.

C

Zone C - Curated Luxury Hot Spring Villas

Exclusive private villas centred around a natural hot spring, offering restorative living experiences that blend luxury, wellness and serenity.

Frequently Asked Questions

LOHAS stands for "Lifestyles of Health and Sustainability." The Richz is designed for a 3-generation community to live, work, learn, and play in a nature-centric environment.

The Richz sits on 41 acres of freehold land in East Genting Highlands.

It is approximately 45 minutes from Kuala Lumpur City Center and 25 minutes from the Gombak Toll.

We possess natural hot spring aquifers measured at 45°C, tested as Class 1 category, which are integrated into our hotels and wellness villas.

The ECRL station, located ~2km away, is expected to be completed by the end of 2026.
