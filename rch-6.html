Frequently Asked Questions

LOHAS stands for "Lifestyles of Health and Sustainability." The Richz is designed for a 3-generation community to live, work, learn, and play in a nature-centric environment.

The Richz sits on 41 acres of freehold land in East Genting Highlands.

It is approximately 45 minutes from Kuala Lumpur City Center and 25 minutes from the Gombak Toll.

We possess natural hot spring aquifers measured at 45°C, tested as Class 1 category, which are integrated into our hotels and wellness villas.

The ECRL station, located ~2km away, is expected to be completed by the end of 2026.
