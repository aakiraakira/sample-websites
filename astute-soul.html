Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking
What should I expect in my first session?

We begin with a brief consultation to discuss your intentions and current state. You will then lie fully clothed on a comfortable massage table while I use light touch or hands-off techniques to assess and clear your energy field. It is deeply relaxing.

Do you offer remote healing options?

Yes, absolutely. Energy is not bound by physical proximity. Remote sessions are conducted via Zoom or phone and are just as potent as in-person visits. Many clients prefer the comfort of receiving healing in their own home.

How often should I come for a session?

This depends entirely on your personal goals. For addressing acute stress or a specific block, we often recommend a series of 3-4 weekly sessions. For ongoing maintenance and spiritual hygiene, once a month is wonderful.

I'm skeptical. Will this still work for me?

Skepticism is healthy! You don't need to "believe" in it for it to work, you just need to be open to the experience. Most skeptics leave their first session feeling surprisingly relaxed and lighter.
