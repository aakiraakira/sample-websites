Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding Executive Condominium eligibility and payment.

Can I buy an EC if I currently own an HDB flat?
Yes, but you must dispose of your current HDB flat within 6 months of collecting the keys to your new EC unit. You must also have fulfilled the Minimum Occupation Period (MOP) of your current flat.

Is there a Resale Levy?
Resale levy applies to second-timer applicants who have previously bought a subsidized flat (HDB/DBSS/EC) directly from HDB/Developer, or received a CPF Housing Grant. The amount varies depending on the type of your first subsidized housing.

What are the payment schemes available?
You can choose between the Normal Payment Scheme (Progressive Payment) or the Deferred Payment Scheme (DPS). The DPS allows you to pay 20% downpayment now and the remaining 80% only upon TOP (Collection of keys), though the purchase price is typically 3% higher.
